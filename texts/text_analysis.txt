Text analysis is a crucial part of Natural Language Processing (NLP) that extracts insights from textual data.

### Challenges in Text Analysis
- **Ambiguity**: Words can have multiple meanings depending on context.
- **Sentiment Detection**: Understanding human emotions in text can be complex.
- **Named Entity Recognition (NER)**: Identifying entities like names and locations.

### Applications of Text Analysis
- **Customer Sentiment Analysis**: Businesses analyze customer reviews to understand satisfaction levels.
- **Fake News Detection**: AI models are used to classify misinformation in online media.
- **Legal Document Processing**: Automating the review of lengthy legal contracts.
